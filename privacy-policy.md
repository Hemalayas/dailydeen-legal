## 1. Introduction

Hemalayas Webcare ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Deen ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our mobile application, available on iOS and Android.

Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

## 2. Summary: Our Core Privacy Commitment

- **We do not sell your data.** Ever.
- **Almost all data is stored locally on your device.** It never leaves your phone unless explicitly described below.
- **Deleting the App deletes your data.** There is no cloud backup.
- **We do not require account registration.**

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

During onboarding and regular use, you may provide:

| Data | Where It's Stored | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| First name | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Personalise the app experience |
| Age range | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Personalise content and experience |
| Daily phone usage hours | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Set focus goals |

| Saved verses and notes | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Personal library |
| Quran reading progress | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Track reading position |
| Daily reminder preferences | Device only (AsyncStorage) | Schedule push notifications |

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

#### Location Data
The App requests access to your device's GPS location **solely** to calculate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction. Location data is processed locally on your device using the Adhan library. **Your location coordinates are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.**

#### Device Permissions Used
- **Location (when in use):** Prayer time calculation and Qibla finder
- **Notifications:** Prayer time alerts and dua reminders
- **Screen Time / App Usage:** Prayer Lock / Focus Mode feature (to block distracting apps)

#### Analytics Data
We use **PostHog** to collect anonymised product analytics. This may include:
- Screens visited and features used
- App session duration
- General usage patterns
- Device type and operating system version

PostHog analytics **do not** include your name, location, mood data, saved verses, or any other personally identifiable information. Analytics are used solely to improve the App.

## 6. Data Storage and Retention

### Local Storage

All core user data (name, preferences, mood history, saved verses, reading progress) is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Is never backed up to our servers
- Is not accessible to Hemalayas Webcare
- Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App

### Cloud / Server Data

Limited anonymised analytics data is processed by PostHog. Verse AI processing may involve anonymised mood/context signals via Supabase edge functions. This data does not contain your name, location, or other identifying information.

Subscription and purchase data is managed by RevenueCat, which retains records as required for billing and dispute resolution.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Deletion

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

- **Access:** Your data is readable within the App (saved verses, mood history, preferences)
- **Delete:** Uninstall the App to permanently delete all locally stored data
- **Modify:** Update your preferences within the App at any time

### Notifications

You may disable push notifications at any time via your device's system settings (Settings → Notifications → Daily Deen).

### Location

You may revoke location access at any time via your device's settings. Revoking location permission will disable prayer time calculation and the Qibla finder.

### Analytics Opt-Out

If you wish to opt out of PostHog analytics, please contact us at [your-support-email@domain.com] and we will assist you.
