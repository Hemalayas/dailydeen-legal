2. Description of the App
Daily Deen is an Islamic spiritual focus and wellness application designed to support Muslim users in their daily religious practice. The App provides the following features:

Prayer Times – Location-based prayer time calculation (Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
Qibla Finder – Compass-based direction toward Makkah
Quran Reader – Browse and read surahs with Arabic text and English translation
Mood Check-In – Daily emotional check-in and mood tracking
Daily Verse – AI-curated Quranic verses based on mood and life context
Saved Library – Save verses with personal notes and mood tags
Prayer Lock / Focus Mode – Block distracting app categories around salah times
Dua Reminders – Scheduled push notifications for duas
Dhikr Counter – Tasbih counter
Wudu Steps – Step-by-step wudu guide
Streak Tracker – Daily engagement streak
Mood Analytics – Visual mood history and weekly/monthly summaries
Journey Dashboard – Progress overview
Home Screen Widget – Prayer time widget
Multilingual Support – English, Arabic, French, Turkish, Malay, Indonesian, Bengali, Urdu
